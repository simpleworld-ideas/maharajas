About

Maharaja’s Technological Institute is the oldest polytechnic college in Kerala. Located in the heart of Thrissur, the cultural capital of the state, if basks in the glory of being the oldest polytechnic in Kerala. More than 900 students pursue their Diploma studies in this prestigious Institution. It offers diploma courses in five different branches of Engineering – Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics and Computer. Around 130 dedicated staff members also work here.

Maharaja’s Technological Institute is a premier technical education center in Kerala targeting the rural people. Even though, the institution is located in the heart of Thrissur town, it is surrounded by a number of villages. This institution serves as Nodal Polytechnic for the Thrissur District in which there are six other Polytechnics. The college has hostel facilities for men and students from distant rural locations, pursue their higher studies here.
